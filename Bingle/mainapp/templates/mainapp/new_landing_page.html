How it Started ?






Bingle originated from the minds of University of Sussex students, initially as a modest academic project. Yet, it quickly evolved into a beacon of sustainability and community for book lovers. Beyond assessment criteria, it now promotes healthy book exchange, fostering connections and saving budgets.

What is Bingle ?



Welcome to Bingle, the friendly book lending app designed to make reading a joy for everyone. In a world where not everyone can afford to buy books or has space to store them, Bingle steps in to bridge the gap. Our mission is simple: to share books among readers, reducing waste and promoting environmental sustainability.

But Bingle isn't just about reading—it's about connecting with others who share your passion for books. Our community is a place where bookworms can come together to share recommendations, discuss their favorite reads, and even make new friends. Whether you're looking for your next literary adventure or eager to share a beloved book with someone else, Bingle is here to help you on your reading journey.

So why not join us at Bingle today? Whether you're a devoted bookworm or just enjoy curling up with a good book now and then, you'll find a warm welcome and endless reading possibilities waiting for you here. Let's rediscover the magic of books together, one page at a time.
